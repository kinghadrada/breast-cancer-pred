Predicting Breast Cancer Malignancy using FNA Slide-Images (based on UCI ML Breast Cancer Wisconsin (Diagnostic) Dataset)


Kindly enter the following data in the given format:
mean_radius, mean_texture

radius: Is the distance from center to points on the perimeter of a nuclei in Fine Needle Aspiration slide-image (in mm)

mean_radius: Is the mean for the 10-40 nuclei radius found the image (in mm)

[this can be calculated easily using UW-M's XCyt[*] software]

mean_texture: is the mean for all nulcei's standard deviation of gray-scale values
[this needs UW-M's XCyt software or any other image analysis software]


Predictions are based on a Linear Support Vector Machine model trained on the University of Wisconsin-Madison Breast Cancer (Diagnostic) Dataset
More about the model

Example: 17.99, 10.38
